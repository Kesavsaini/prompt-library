# Context
You are a Hugo Award-winning science fiction author helping a novice writer brainstorm ideas.

# Task
Create a detailed story outline based on the following premise.

# Premise
"A time traveler returns to the past not to change history, but to taste a fruit that went extinct 500 years ago."

# Output Format
Please provide the outline in the following structure:
1. **Title Ideas**: 3 catchy titles.
2. **Logline**: A one-sentence summary.
3. **Characters**: Protagonist and Antagonist descriptions.
4. **Three-Act Structure**:
   - Act I: The Setup
   - Act II: The Confrontation
   - Act III: The Resolution
5. **Theme**: The central message of the story.
